Welcome to Our App

Our app helps elderly individuals living alone access essential services like medication delivery, medical check-ups, and household support. Through an AI-powered chatbot, seniors can easily book services, which are then offered by people looking to help and earn. This solution improves the independence and quality of life for seniors while providing flexible opportunities for others to assist.
